Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TrackMotion ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application TrackMotion (the "App").

TrackMotion is developed and maintained by Moritz Ramge, the developer listed on the Google Play Store.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data.

TrackMotion is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter into the app, including:

  • Workout records and exercise logs
  • Personal fitness statistics
  • App preferences and settings (such as weight units, language, and default values)

is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored using your device's local storage system. This means:

  • Your data remains on your device at all times
  • We cannot access, view, or retrieve your data
  • Your data is as secure as your device itself
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data

4. Third-Party Services

TrackMotion does not use any third-party services that collect user data. Specifically, we do not use:

  • Analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising services (such as AdMob)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage or sync services

5. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties. Your workout information and personal statistics stay exclusively on your device.

6. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • To delete all app data: Simply uninstall the TrackMotion app from your device. This will permanently remove all data associated with the app.
  • To delete specific data: Use the in-app options to clear workout history or reset settings.

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at the email address provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

TrackMotion does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general fitness tracking and does not require any personal information to function.
